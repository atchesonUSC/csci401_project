Welcome and Consent


To check in to your appointment, you may tap the Check-In button.



Next, you will be asked if you would like to consent to
participating in a telemedicine consultation.

You may use your finger to scroll up and down
on your device screen to review the consent form.



If you would like to consent to telemedicine consultation
and proceed with your appointment, you may
use your finger to tap the Sign button.



To verify your consent, you may draw your signature
on the dashed line ("sign here") using your finger.

To redo your signature, you may tap the Clear button.
When complete, you may press the Sign button.



Press "Next" to learn more!
