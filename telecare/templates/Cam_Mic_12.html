Enable Camera Walkthrough (Android)


To use the camera for your upcoming telehealth
appointment, please follow the below steps.



Step 1: Go to the settings app on your
device and tap the icon to open it.





Step 2: In the settings app, scroll until you
see the USC TeleCARE icon (should be
toward the bottom of the page).





Step 3: Tap the arrow next to the icon.





Step 4: Tap the toggle next to "camera"
to enable it.





The toggle should turn green when the
camera is enabled:
