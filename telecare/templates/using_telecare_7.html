Computer Setup


We can now setup the computer's microphone and camera so that your physician can both see and hear you during your visit.

We will also test the internet connection.






Step 1: Setup Microphone — Click on the downward pointing arrow
in the white box below where it shows a picture of a microphone.

Click on a choice of microphone. You can try different choices.





Tip: If the gray blocks change color when you talk, then
your microphone is working!

Step 2: Setup Camera — Click on the downward pointing arrow
in the white box below where it shows a picture of a camera.

Click on a choice of camera. You can try different choices.





Tip: If your image appears on the computer screen, then
your camera is working!

Step 3:Test Connection — Click on the blue Test connection
button to check your connection.
