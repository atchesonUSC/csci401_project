How to Check Your Internet


Since the USC TeleCARE service uses internet for your appointment, it's important to make sure that you have a good internet connection at the location from where you'd like to connect to your appointment, such as from your home.


To check that you have a good internet connection, please call our USC TeleCARE Support Team at least two days prior to your appointment, and we'll help!
