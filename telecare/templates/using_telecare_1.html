Opening USC TeleCARE


For accessing your appointment on USC TeleCARE using your
computer, please follow the below steps.



Step 1: Visit your email inbox. Search for a USC TeleCARE
appointment message having subject "Check-in online".

Click on the Enter Waiting Room button at the bottom.





You will be directed to USC TeleCARE for checking in
to your appointment.


Step 2: On the newly opened page (which looks like the below picture),
you may click the Check-In button to begin the check-in process.
