Testing Your Connection


Important!

Once you have reached this point, please DO NOT leave the
USC TeleCARE app.

Leaving the app can affect the connection to your appointment.




You will now be placed in your appointment waiting room. Your
physician will connect to your waiting room once they are ready.






While in the waiting room, you may test your internet connection.

To do so, you may press the blue Test Your Connection button.

If the below message appears, tap the OK button so that
your physician will be able to hear you.




If the below message appears, tap the OK button so that
your physician will be able to see you.
