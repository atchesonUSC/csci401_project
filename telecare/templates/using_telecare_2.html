Opening USC TeleCARE


If you would like to connect to your appointment using a smartphone or tablet, you may do so by following one of the below approaches.



Option 1: Email


Step 1: If you do not have the USC TeleCARE app installed, or if you are
unsure, please review the Installing USC TeleCARE lesson.

Step 2: Visit your email inbox. Search for a USC TeleCARE
appointment message having subject "Check-in online".



Step 3: Using your finger, tap on the Enter Waiting Room
button at the bottom of the email.

Step 4: A new page will automatically open, as shown below.



Step 5: After pressing Confirm, a message will appear. Press the word
Open to begin your appointment in the USC TeleCARE app.




Option 2: Text Message


Step 1: Visit your text messaging app and look for a message from
the following phone number → (855) 849 - 1058



Step 2: Using your finger, press on the blue text. A new page will
automatically open. From here, please refer to steps 4 and 5 from above.
